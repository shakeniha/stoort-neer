Frequently Asked Questions

How do I purchase Stoort Neer's music?

You can purchase or stream the music directly via our Bandcamp page or through various digital platforms like Spotify and Apple Music.

Is there a physical release of the album?

Currently, Stoort Neer offers digital downloads. If a physical release becomes available, updates will be shared on our social media and website.

What formats are available for download?

Our music is available for download in MP3, FLAC, and other high-quality formats via Bandcamp.

Returns Policy

Can I return or refund a digital download?

Due to the nature of digital content, all sales are final. Once purchased, digital music cannot be returned or refunded.

What if I experience technical issues with my download?

If you encounter any issues with downloading or accessing your purchase, please contact our support team at support@stoortneer.com, and we will assist you in resolving the issue.

How can I contact Stoort Neer for additional questions?

You can reach us via email at contact@stoortneer.com, and we'll respond as soon as possible.
